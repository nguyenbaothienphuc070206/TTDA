# Final Pitch Positioning (Judge-facing)

## Core Question

Why use this instead of YouTube?

## One-line Answer

Vovinam Learning is not just content playback. It is a structured, AI-powered training system with live form feedback, progress intelligence, and social accountability.

## Signature Feature (WOW)

- AI Form Check with camera feedback
- Immediate correction prompts while user practices
- Integrated into streak, progress, and leaderboard loops

## Product Positioning

- Learning layer: structured belt roadmap
- Tracking layer: measurable progress + consistency
- Community layer: realtime social motivation
- AI layer: memory-aware coach and personalized plans
- Business layer: subscription + affiliate + coach/B2B expansion

## Business Realism

1. Freemium

- Core lessons and basic tracking

2. Premium

- AI coach pro

- Video/form analysis

3. B2B

- Club dashboard for coaches
- Cohort monitoring and feedback workflows

- Future-ready: computer vision service + queue processing
- Security: RLS + route rate limits + payload validation

Vovinam Learning is not just an app. It is a structured, AI-powered training system that transforms traditional martial arts into a scalable, data-driven experience.
